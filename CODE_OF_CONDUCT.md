# Code of Conduct

Julewire follows [The Ruby Community Conduct Guideline](https://www.ruby-lang.org/en/conduct)
in all collaborative spaces, including issue threads, pull requests, review
comments, and project discussions:

- Participants will be tolerant of opposing views.
- Participants must ensure that their language and actions are free of personal
  attacks and disparaging personal remarks.
- When interpreting the words and actions of others, participants should assume
  good intentions.
- Behaviour which can be reasonably considered harassment will not be tolerated.

Concerns about behaviour within this project can be sent to
[sl.bug.sl@gmail.com](mailto:sl.bug.sl@gmail.com).
